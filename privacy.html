Privacy Policy


1 Stop ConEd LLC respects the privacy and confidentiality of visitors and users of www.1StopConEd.com. This privacy statement explains our practices governing information obtained for our continuing education courses. By visiting www.1StopConEd.com, you accept the practices described in this Privacy Statement.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy
We are committed to protecting your privacy. You have chosen to do business with us, and we recognize our obligation to keep the information you provide secure and to inform you of our policies concerning its use. You can be sure that our commitment to protect your information will continue under the principles and online guidelines described below.

Information We Collect
We only collect information that is essential for our operations and necessary to properly submit your continuing education credits to the Michigan Department of Licensing and Regulatory Affairs. We collect two types of information:

  1. Personal information. 1 Stop ConEd LLC does not collect personally identifiable information about a user unless it is voluntarily and knowingly provided to us and so that we can serve the user’s needs and our legitimate business purposes. In order to provide you with credit for completing one of our home study real estate continuing courses, the State of Michigan requires that we collect and maintain the following information: first and last name, street address, city, state, zip code, telephone number, email address, last four digits of Social Security number,and Michigan real estate license number. This information is collected when you complete our new student/customer registration form. It is required to process your continuing education credit and for us to send you important communications.
  2. Standard web server traffic pattern information. General traffic, website usage, and length of stay information may also be collected. We may use such data as a form of aggregate information to anonymously determine how much time visitors spend on each page of our website, how visitors navigate throughout the website and how we may tailor our web pages to better meet the needs of visitors. This information may be used to improve our website and is not shared with any third-party. With the exception of your IP address, which is logged when you place an order on our website, technical information is not routinely associated with your personal data.
  3. Information about your course completions. In order for our systems to properly process your course purchase and completion, we may store data about time of purchase, quiz attempts, accesses to the quiz or other 1StopConEd features, and when you have been certified.

Disclosure of Information to Outside Parties
Except as specifically stated below, we do not share any information collected through this website with third parties. We disclose your information in only four situations:

  1. To the Michigan Department of Licensing and Regulatory Affairs, as required to report real estate continuing education credits you earn after taking our home study con ed courses;
  2. To our payment processor (Stripe), so it can validate your credit card and banking transaction. All financial transactions are carried out through Stripe using a Secure Socket Layer (SSL) that encrypts the data you provide to ensure privacy. SSL is the industry standard for securing web-based transactions;
  3. In response to a subpoena, court order, legal process, or to parties authorized by law to access your personal information; and
  4. In order to enforce the website’s policies, and to protect and defend the rights or property of 1 Stop ConEd LLC.

We will never sell, exchange, disclose, or otherwise distribute your personal information to any other third-parties.

Email Communications
When you make a purchase on our website, you are communicating with us electronically. Once you do so, you consent to receive occasional emails from us relating to (1) Michigan real estate continuing education requirements and deadlines and (2) special offers and discounts on our Michigan real estate continuing education courses. You may choose to stop receiving those emails by following the unsubscribe instructions included in those emails or you can contact us at support@1StopConEd.com. As highlighted above, we will never sell, exchange, disclose, or otherwise distribute your personal information to any other third-parties.

How We Protect Your Information
We are committed to protecting the security of your personal information. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we strive to maintain physical, electronic, and administrative safeguards. 1 Stop ConEd LLC data is stored in password-controlled servers. Our staff is educated about the importance of safeguarding your information and we are committed to holding them accountable for the protection of your confidentiality.

We Do Not Store Credit Card Information
This website does not store credit card information. All financial transactions are carried out through a seamless integration of Stripe using a Secure Socket Layer (SSL) that encrypts the data you provide to ensure privacy. SSL is the industry standard for securing web-based transactions.

Links to Other Websites
Our privacy policies apply only to your use of the www.1StopConEd.com website. This website contains links to third-party websites and resources. We do not disclose personally identifiable information to those third-party linked websites, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices. Links to other websites do not imply an endorsement of the materials or policies on those websites. You should read the privacy policies of each website you visit to determine what information that website may be collecting about you.

Updates to Privacy Statement

If that happens, we will post the revised policy here. Revisions are effective upon posting and your continued use of this website after the posting will be subject to the changed terms of the revisions.
